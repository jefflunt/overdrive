# Todos Overview

Todos are your project's to-do list, designed to keep you and the AI organized. They track pending tasks, future ideas, and known issues, ensuring nothing falls through the cracks.

## Managing Your Work

The Todo system integrates with your workflow to provide a clear picture of what needs to be done.

### Types of Todos

*   **Native Todos**: Tasks you add yourself directly in the overdrive dashboard.
*   **Jira Integration**: If configured, overdrive can sync your tasks with a Jira project, allowing you to pick up Jira issues as engineering jobs.

### Features

*   **Auto-Save**: All changes to your todos are automatically saved as you type, so you never lose progress.
*   **Starring**: You can "star" important tasks to highlight them in the list.
*   **Subtask Management**: Break down complex tasks into smaller subtasks for better organization.
*   **Status Sync**: When you submit a todo as an engineering job, its status is automatically updated (e.g., to "In Progress" or "Done") based on the job's outcome.

### Getting Started
Navigate to the `/todos` page to view your current list. You can add new items, update statuses, or just review what the AI has found in your codebase.

Head back to the Help Home if you need assistance with specific Todo features!
