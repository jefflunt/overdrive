# Todos Overview

The Todo system is your project's organization tool, helping you and the AI manage pending tasks, future features, and known issues. It provides a clear overview of the work required to complete a project.

## Task Management

The Todo dashboard provides a hierarchical view of your project's roadmap:

### Todo Types

*   **Native Todos**: Managed directly within overdrive, these tasks are stored locally for fast access.
*   **Jira Integration**: If configured, overdrive synchronizes your tasks with an external Jira project, allowing you to manage enterprise issues within your local workflow.

### Key Features

*   **Hierarchical Subtasks**: Break down complex features into smaller, manageable subtasks for better organization.
*   **Starring**: Highlight critical or high-priority tasks to make them stand out in the dashboard.
*   **Auto-Save**: Changes to titles and descriptions are automatically saved as you type, ensuring you never lose progress.
*   **Status Synchronization**: When a todo is submitted as an engineering job, its status automatically updates (e.g., to "In Progress" or "Done") based on the job's outcome.

## Workflow

Managing tasks within the Todo system involves several straightforward steps:

1.  **Add**: Create new root tasks or nested subtasks to map out your project.
2.  **Organize**: Use starring and subtask nesting to prioritize your workflow.
3.  **Submit**: Convert draft tasks into active engineering jobs directly from the dashboard.
4.  **Review**: Watch the status of your tasks as the AI completes its assignments.
